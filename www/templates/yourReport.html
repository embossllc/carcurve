What is CPM (Cost-Per-Mile)?

Your CPM is the "Price" divided by the "Mileage". We do not include, maintenance, repairs, insurance, inflation ect...only the principal cost and miles driven.

How do I find out my Sales Price or Sales Milage?

Start by doing some research to get an estimate.

  • Search the internet for your vehicle's year, make, model & trim.
  • Find a similar vehicle for sale at the mileage you would like to sell at.
  • Find an average price of 3-4 similar vehicles.
  • Determine how many years you will drive the vehicle by dividing your approx. total mileage by 15000 (average annual driving milage is 15k miles).
  • If you want to get a better estimate, tack on additional expenses and depreciation.
  • Use CarCurve to get quick results!
